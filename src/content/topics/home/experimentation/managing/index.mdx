## Overview

This section explains how to start and stop experiment iterations, interpret experiment results, and customize which parts of your user base are exposed to experiments.

After you start recording an experiment, it's time to watch the data roll in. You can see everything your experiment is doing in the Experiments tab belonging to the flag contributing to the experiment, or on the Experiments dashboard.
